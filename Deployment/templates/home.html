About the Model

Understanding Your Energy Profile

Our clustering model uses K-Prototypes algorithm to analyze your household energy profile and group you with similar users. The model analyzes the following factors:

  • Country
  • Energy Source
  • Monthly Energy Usage (kWh)
  • Year of Data
  • Household Size
  • Income Level
  • Urban/Rural Location
  • Energy Adoption Year
  • Subsidy Status
  • Cost Savings (USD)

The 10 Energy User Groups

  • 1 Heavy Users in Underserved Areas: High consumption with limited infrastructure access
  • 2 High Impact Rural Households: Rural settings with significant potential for improvement
  • 3 Minimal Users with Strong Means: Low usage paired with high financial capacity
  • 4 Urban Subsidized Adopters: City dwellers taking advantage of government programs
  • 5 Budget-Conscious Urban Beneficiaries: Cost-sensitive urban households
  • 6 Efficient Urban Savers: City residents with optimized energy efficiency
  • 7 High Consumers with Low Returns: Households using significant energy with poor outcomes
  • 8 Frugal Households with High Gains: Modest means achieving excellent results
  • 9 Urban Energy Spenders: City households with above-average consumption
  • 10 Wealthy Subsidized Consumers: High-income households benefiting from incentives
